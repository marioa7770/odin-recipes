Chicken Marsala

About this dish

If you like chicken and wine with mushrooms then this is the perfect dish for you, make it with pasta on the side with garlic bread

Ingredients

  • Chicken Thighs
  • Mushrooms
  • Red Wine
  • Pasta Noodles
  • Dash Of Salt
  • Dash Of Pepper
  • Minced Garlic
  • Onions
  • Olive Oil

Steps

    Sautee onions and garlic
    Cut chicken thighs into cutlets
    Boil hot water and add pasta
